Privacy policy

How DeductIQ handles your data.

Effective date: March 22, 2026

1. What we collect

DeductIQ collects minimal data required to operate the bot:

  • Discord User ID — used for rate limiting and user preferences. This is a public numeric identifier, not your email or password.
  • Command usage — which commands you use and when, for rate limiting and anonymous usage analytics.
  • State preference — if you set a home state, we store your two-letter state abbreviation.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or address
  • Your income, trading activity, or financial data
  • Social Security numbers or tax identification numbers
  • Bank account or credit card information
  • The content of your messages in Discord (we only see slash command inputs)

3. How we use your data

  • Rate limiting — tracking daily command usage to enforce free tier limits
  • Service improvement — anonymous, aggregated analytics on which commands are popular
  • Personalization — storing your state preference so you don't have to type it each time

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, license, or share your data with any third party, data broker, advertising network, or monetization service. Your slash command inputs are sent to Anthropic's API for AI processing — Anthropic's privacy policy applies to that processing. No other third parties receive your data.

5. Data retention

  • Usage tracking data (rate limiting) — automatically deleted after 30 days
  • User preferences (state, settings) — stored until you request deletion

6. Your rights

You can request deletion of all your data at any time by using the /delete-my-data command in Discord, or by contacting us at privacy@deductiq.xyz. We will delete your data within 7 days of the request.

7. Data security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to the bot's service account. We follow industry-standard security practices.

DeductIQ is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
